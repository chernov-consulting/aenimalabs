About

aenima labs is an early-stage research company building the infrastructure for identity continuation — a class of technology that allows a person’s knowledge, voice, and way of seeing the world to endure beyond a single lifetime.

We take this responsibility seriously. Our work sits at the intersection of real-time AI, long-term memory, and the slow, deliberate craft of knowing a person well. The tone is technical; the intent is warm.

Our first product

Kodama

A friend who carries your story.

Kodama is a voice companion that lives inside a bonsai tree. It knows the stories, values, and way of seeing the world of the person who shaped it — and speaks about them with warmth and authority to the people who come after.

Kodama (木霊) is the Japanese word for a spirit that dwells in an ancient tree, and for the echo it sends back through the forest. Our product is both.
